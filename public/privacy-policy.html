Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your health information

NHS QIP Initiative
Effective Date: 28th June 2025

1. Who We Are

OncsCare is a digital symptom monitoring app developed by Dr Sheraz Azhar (Medical Oncology Registrar, UHL) as part of an NHS-led QIP initiative to support outpatient cancer care.

This app is being used as part of a QIP project approved by University Hospitals of Leicester NHS Trust, with appropriate ethics and information governance approvals. The app is not currently available for public download or commercial use.

2. Purpose of the App

OncsCare enables cancer patients receiving outpatient treatment to securely log their daily symptoms. This information is reviewed by their clinical team to help detect signs of deterioration earlier and support timely care.

3. What Data We Collect

  • Health-related information: Daily symptom entries (e.g., pain, nausea, fever)
  • Usage information: Frequency of app use, timestamped log entries
  • Device information (minimal): Device type and operating system version
  • No location, contact, or camera data is collected

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To support clinical care during outpatient cancer treatment
  • To evaluate the feasibility, acceptability, and effectiveness of digital symptom monitoring
  • To train a predictive model (in future phases), using pseudonymised data only

Your data is never sold or shared for commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is encrypted during transmission and stored on secure NHS-approved servers. We comply with UK GDPR and NHS Digital data protection standards.

Only authorised members of the NHS QIP team have access to identifiable information, and only where necessary.
