Important Information

Emergency Contact

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, do not contact us through email. Instead:

  • Call 999 for emergency services
  • Contact your clinical team directly
  • Visit your nearest emergency department
  • Use your hospital's emergency helpline

Secure Communication

All communications regarding your health data and study participation are handled through secure NHS email systems. Please do not send sensitive health information through non-secure channels.

Research Study Details

OncsCare is part of an ongoing NHS research initiative to improve cancer care through digital health monitoring. The study is:

  • Approved by University Hospitals of Leicester NHS Trust
  • Reviewed by NHS research ethics committee
  • Compliant with NHS Digital information governance standards
  • Conducted in accordance with UK GDPR requirements
